Best films according to Beth

My top three movies of all time

Fifth Element

The visuals, the costumes, the music, all combining together to make a work of art.

The Proposal

My 'pick-me-up' film when I'm sad. This never fails to make me laugh outloud.

Maleficent

This film is beautiful in a way that I find completely compelling. Some great acting (apart from that fake British accent, Angelina).
